Acme Pharma competitor pipeline overview. Our lead investigational asset is in Phase 2 development for chronic obstructive pulmonary disease. Enrollment is ongoing across 40 clinical trial sites in North America and Europe. Topline efficacy data are expected in the second half of next year. The program targets the IL-33 inflammatory pathway. No new safety signals have been reported to date. The company also continues to advance two earlier-stage assets in asthma and chronic rhinosinusitis. A regulatory filing for the lead program is anticipated within two years, pending positive results. Management reaffirmed its commitment to the respiratory portfolio at the most recent investor day and outlined plans to expand manufacturing capacity ahead of a potential launch.
