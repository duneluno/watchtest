Acme Pharma competitor pipeline overview. Our lead investigational asset is currently in Phase 2 development for chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD). Enrollment is ongoing across 40 clinical trial sites in North America and Europe, with a target of 600 patients. Topline efficacy data are expected in the second half of next year. The program targets the IL-33 inflammatory pathway, a mechanism increasingly central to respiratory drug development. No new safety signals have been reported to date, and the independent data monitoring committee has recommended the study continue without modification. The company also continues to advance two earlier-stage assets, one in severe asthma and another in chronic rhinosinusitis with nasal polyps. A regulatory filing for the lead program is anticipated within two years, pending positive pivotal results. Management reaffirmed its commitment to the respiratory portfolio at the most recent investor day and outlined plans to expand manufacturing capacity ahead of a potential commercial launch.
